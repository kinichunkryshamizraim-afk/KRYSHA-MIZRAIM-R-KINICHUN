Personal Biography

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Kinichun Krysha Mizraim, a vibrant 19-year-old who brings joy and creativity to everything she does. As the first child of Eva and Marvin Kinichun, I have grown into a wonderful young woman with diverse interests and a passion for life's simple pleasures.

Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Criminology at the University of the Cordilleras, I am passionate about criminal justice and public safety. My academic journey has been focused on understanding the complexities of criminal behavior, law enforcement procedures, and the justice system.

I am committed to making a positive impact in the field of criminology while maintaining a balanced life filled with poetry, photography, and the beautiful moments that make life worth living. Through my studies, I have developed strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of criminal justice principles that I aim to apply in my future career.

About My Passions

My interests and hobbies reflect my appreciation for beauty, creativity, and life's simple pleasures. Photography, especially during golden hours, allows me to capture the world's natural beauty and practice skills that complement my forensic studies. Creating and reading poetry nurtures my creative side and enhances my communication skills.

Staying active through badminton and jogging keeps me physically fit and mentally sharp, which is essential for my demanding criminology studies. These sports also teach me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance - qualities that will serve me well in law enforcement.

Traveling opens my mind to different cultures and perspectives, which is invaluable for understanding diverse communities I may serve in my future career. Whether I'm exploring new places, experimenting with makeup, enjoying delicious food, or getting lost in a captivating K-drama, each activity contributes to my personal growth and brings balance to my academic life.

These hobbies help me maintain a healthy work-life balance and remind me that there's beauty to be found in everyday moments, from a peaceful morning jog to discovering a new poem that speaks to my soul.
